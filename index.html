Q-ACE Awards and recognitions

Presidential Early Career Award for Scientists and Engineers (January 2025)

Presented to Justyna P. Zwolak

“For the development of fundamental and applied advances at the intersection of machine learning and quantum information science, pushing forward both the state-of-the-art in automating challenging quantum computing experiments and providing a new foundation for high-dimensional optimization problems.”

2024 Department of Commerce Bronze Medal Award (January 2025)

Presented to Justyna P. Zwolak and Jacob M. Taylor

“For pioneering the field of machine learning for quantum control.”

ITL Best Journal Paper of the Year Award (July 2024)

Presented to Justyna P. Zwolak

For your jointly authored paper Colloquium: Advances in automation of quantum dot devices control published in Reviews of Modern Physics in February 2023.

2024 Award for Excellence in Research in Applied Mathematics (May 2024)

Presented to Justyna P. Zwolak

“For outstanding contributions in the application of machine learning to the control of systems at the frontiers of quantum science and technology.”

GDS IMPACT Award for Excellence in Graduate Research (February 2024)

Presented to Harry S. Chalfin

To recognize the noteworthy progress in academic career at the intersection of Physics and Data Science.
